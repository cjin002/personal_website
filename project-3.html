Technological and Cultural Integration Poster Series

By: Yang(Cindy) Jin, September 2023

Description

In "Take Control Over the Future," I present a visual symphony of my insights on cultural immersion and the exhilarating pace of technological advancement. Narrating my experiences as an international student, I have utilized the potent tools of Adobe InDesign, Illustrator, and Photoshop to articulate the nuances of identity and the thrill of discovery in different graphic design techniques. While the core message of my art remains steadfast across the series, the varied graphic styles reflect my versatility and adaptability in design. The bold typography interlacing with complex graphics symbolizes my philosophy: to embrace the beauty of our diverse world and harness the power of technology for a future we can all influence. This collection is more than art; it is a conversation between the past and the possible, a first-person account of learning to appreciate myriad perspectives and aspiring to a tomorrow rich with endless potential.
