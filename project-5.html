Toothpaste Tube User Experience Research and Redesign

June 2023

By: Yang(Cindy) Jin, Zhaorui Zhang, Cancan Huang, Hailey Ye, Zainab Jimoh, Jelani Lizarraga

Description

In this project, our group tackled the everyday challenge of toothpaste tube design with a focus on enhancing user experience. Through rigorous user research and iterative prototyping, we identified key issues such as cap loss and toothpaste accessibility. Our solution, a transparent pump-dispenser toothpaste tube, directly addresses these concerns by improving visibility of the remaining product and simplifying the extraction process. This user-centric approach to redesign enabled us to refine our prototype into a practical and intuitive product that promises to transform the daily routine of dental hygiene into a seamless, waste-reducing ritual.
